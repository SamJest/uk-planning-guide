Wraparound Extension Planning Rules in Lambeth vs National Permitted Development

UK permitted development legislation allows many wraparound extension projects without planning permission provided certain limits are followed.

However local planning authorities such as those covering Lambeth may impose additional planning controls through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions.

Do I need planning permission for wraparound extension in Lambeth?

How close can a wraparound extension be to a boundary?

Do conservation areas affect planning permission?
