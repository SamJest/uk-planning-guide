Understanding Maximum Height Rules

Planning permission rules relating to maximum height rules can affect whether developments qualify under permitted development rights.

Many types of home improvement projects such as extensions, loft conversions and garden rooms may be built without planning permission provided development limits are followed.

However local planning authorities may impose additional planning restrictions through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions which remove permitted development rights.
