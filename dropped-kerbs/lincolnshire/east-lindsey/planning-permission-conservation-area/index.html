Understanding Planning Permission in Conservation Areas

If you are planning a dropped kerb project in East Lindsey, it is important to understand planning rules relating to planning permission in conservation areas.

These rules determine whether a development can proceed under permitted development or whether a planning application may be required.

Dropped Kerb Planning Rules in East Lindsey vs National Permitted Development

UK permitted development legislation allows many dropped kerb projects without planning permission provided certain limits are followed.

However local planning authorities such as those covering East Lindsey may impose additional planning controls through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions.
