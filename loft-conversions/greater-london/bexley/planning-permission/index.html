Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need planning permission for a loft conversion in Bexley?

Many loft conversion projects fall under permitted development rules, but planning permission may be required if the structure exceeds height limits, is located in a conservation area, or if permitted development rights have been removed.

What size loft conversion can be built without planning permission?

Outbuildings such as loft conversions must normally remain within permitted development limits relating to height, location within the garden and total coverage of land around the property.

Does being near a boundary affect planning permission?

Yes. Structures located within 2 metres of a boundary usually have a maximum height limit of 2.5 metres under permitted development rules.
