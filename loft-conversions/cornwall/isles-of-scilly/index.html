Loft Conversion Planning Permission in Isles of Scilly

Planning guidance for loft conversion in Isles of Scilly, Cornwall.

Permitted Development

Outbuildings may be permitted development subject to national permitted development limits.

Maximum Height

Maximum overall height of 4 metres with a dual-pitched roof, or 3 metres otherwise.

Boundary Rules

Height limited to 2.5 metres if within 2 metres of a boundary.
