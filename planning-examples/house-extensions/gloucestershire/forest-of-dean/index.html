House Extension Planning Example – Forest of Dean

This example explains how a typical house extensions planning application was assessed by the local planning authority covering Forest of Dean, Gloucestershire.

Example Planning Application

A homeowner proposed building a house extensions with a maximum height of 3m located approximately 1.5m from the property boundary.

The development was reviewed against national permitted development rules and local planning policies.

Planning Decision

The application was approved because the development complied with height limits, boundary distance requirements and design guidance.

Key Planning Considerations

  • Height limits under permitted development
  • Distance from property boundaries
  • Impact on neighbouring properties
  • Local planning authority policies
