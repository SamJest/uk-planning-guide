Single Storey Extension Planning Rules in Test Valley vs National Permitted Development

UK permitted development legislation allows many single storey extension projects without planning permission provided certain limits are followed.

However local planning authorities such as those covering Test Valley may impose additional planning controls through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions.

Planning Permission Questions in Test Valley

Do I need planning permission for single storey extension in Test Valley?

Many single storey extension projects in Test Valley may fall under permitted development rights if they remain within national planning limits.

What height can a single storey extension be without planning permission?

Single storey extensions must not exceed 4m in overall height and must keep eaves height below 3m if within 2m of a boundary.

How close can a single storey extension be to a boundary?

Extensions must not extend beyond the principal elevation of the house facing the highway.

Do conservation areas affect planning permission?

Yes. Additional planning restrictions may apply in conservation areas or where Article 4 directions remove permitted development rights.
