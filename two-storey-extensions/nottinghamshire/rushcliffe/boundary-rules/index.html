Boundary Rules Questions

Do I need planning permission for two storey extension projects in Rushcliffe?

Many two storey extension developments in Rushcliffe may fall under permitted development rights if the project remains within national planning limits.

What height limits apply to two storey extension developments?

Outbuildings with pitched roofs may normally reach up to 4 metres in height, while flat roof structures are typically limited to 3 metres.

Do conservation areas affect two storey extension planning rules?

Yes. Additional planning restrictions may apply within conservation areas or where Article 4 directions remove permitted development rights.
