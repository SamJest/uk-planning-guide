Garden Room Planning Permission

This guide explains UK planning rules relating to garden room planning permission affecting common home improvement projects.

Planning Guidance

Planning permission requirements depend on the type of development, property location and whether permitted development rights apply.

Many home improvement projects such as extensions, loft conversions and outbuildings can be built without planning permission provided permitted development limits are followed.
