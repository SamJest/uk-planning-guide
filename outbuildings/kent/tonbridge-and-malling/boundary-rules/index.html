Boundary Rules for Outbuildings Projects

Understanding boundary rules is important when planning a outbuildings project in Tonbridge and Malling. National planning rules set limits on size, height and location of developments that may be built under permitted development rights.

Local planning policies, conservation areas and Article 4 directions may also affect whether planning permission is required.
