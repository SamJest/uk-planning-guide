Permitted Development in Conservation Areas for Change of Use Projects

Understanding permitted development in conservation areas is important when planning a change of use project in Derbyshire Dales. National planning rules set limits on size, height and location of developments that may be built under permitted development rights.

Local planning policies, conservation areas and Article 4 directions may also affect whether planning permission is required.

Change of Use Planning Rules in Derbyshire Dales vs National Permitted Development

UK permitted development legislation allows many change of use projects without planning permission provided certain limits are followed.

However local planning authorities such as those covering Derbyshire Dales may impose additional planning controls through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions.
