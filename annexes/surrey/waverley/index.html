Annexe Planning Rules in Waverley vs National Permitted Development

UK permitted development legislation allows many annexe projects without planning permission provided certain limits are followed.

However local planning authorities such as those covering Waverley may impose additional planning controls through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions.
