Understanding UK Planning Permission

Many home improvement projects fall under permitted development rights, meaning planning permission may not be required if specific size and design limits are followed.

However, planning permission may still be required where properties are located in conservation areas, where Article 4 directions apply, or where developments exceed permitted development limits.

UK Planning Guide summarises these rules to help homeowners understand the key planning considerations before starting a project.
