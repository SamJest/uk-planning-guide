UK Planning Permission Guidance for Home Improvements

UK Planning Guide provides clear guidance on planning permission rules for common home improvement projects including loft conversions, extensions, garden rooms and outbuildings.

Our structured guidance explains national permitted development rules and links to official council planning resources so homeowners can understand planning requirements before starting a project.
