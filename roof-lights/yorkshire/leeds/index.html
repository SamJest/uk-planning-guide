Rooflight Planning Rules in Leeds vs National Permitted Development

UK permitted development legislation allows many rooflight projects without planning permission provided certain limits are followed.

However local planning authorities such as those covering Leeds may impose additional planning controls through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions.
