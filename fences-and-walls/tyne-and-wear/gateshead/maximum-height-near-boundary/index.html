Understanding Maximum Height Near Boundary

If you are planning a fence and wall project in Gateshead, it is important to understand planning rules relating to maximum height near boundary.

These rules determine whether a development can proceed under permitted development or whether a planning application may be required.

Fence and Wall Planning Rules in Gateshead vs National Permitted Development

UK permitted development legislation allows many fence and wall projects without planning permission provided certain limits are followed.

However local planning authorities such as those covering Gateshead may impose additional planning controls through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions.
