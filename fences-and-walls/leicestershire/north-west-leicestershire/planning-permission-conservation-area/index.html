Planning Permission in Conservation Areas for Fence and Wall Projects

Understanding planning permission in conservation areas is important when planning a fence and wall project in North West Leicestershire. National planning rules set limits on size, height and location of developments that may be built under permitted development rights.

Local planning policies, conservation areas and Article 4 directions may also affect whether planning permission is required.

Fence and Wall Planning Rules in North West Leicestershire vs National Permitted Development

UK permitted development legislation allows many fence and wall projects without planning permission provided certain limits are followed.

However local planning authorities such as those covering North West Leicestershire may impose additional planning controls through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions.
