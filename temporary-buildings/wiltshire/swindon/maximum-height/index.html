Temporary Building Planning Rules in Swindon vs National Permitted Development

UK permitted development legislation allows many temporary building projects without planning permission provided certain limits are followed.

However local planning authorities such as those covering Swindon may impose additional planning controls through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions.

Maximum Height Rules Questions

Do I need planning permission for temporary building projects in Swindon?

Many temporary building developments in Swindon may fall under permitted development rights if the project remains within national planning limits.

What height limits apply to temporary building developments?

Outbuildings with pitched roofs may normally reach up to 4 metres in height, while flat roof structures are typically limited to 3 metres.

Do conservation areas affect temporary building planning rules?

Yes. Additional planning restrictions may apply within conservation areas or where Article 4 directions remove permitted development rights.
