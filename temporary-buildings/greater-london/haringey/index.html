Temporary Building Planning Rules in Haringey vs National Permitted Development

UK permitted development legislation allows many temporary building projects without planning permission provided certain limits are followed.

However local planning authorities such as those covering Haringey may impose additional planning controls through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions.
