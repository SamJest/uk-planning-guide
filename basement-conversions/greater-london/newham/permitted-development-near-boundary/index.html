Permitted Development Near Boundary for Basement Conversion Projects

Understanding permitted development near boundary is important when planning a basement conversion project in Newham. National planning rules set limits on size, height and location of developments that may be built under permitted development rights.

Local planning policies, conservation areas and Article 4 directions may also affect whether planning permission is required.

Basement Conversion Planning Rules in Newham vs National Permitted Development

UK permitted development legislation allows many basement conversion projects without planning permission provided certain limits are followed.

However local planning authorities such as those covering Newham may impose additional planning controls through conservation area policies or Article 4 directions.
